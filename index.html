Telemedicine


As the healthcare landscape is changing and evolving, we envision millions of people living healthy connected lives from the comfort of their home barring distance and time. Telemedicine is the remote delivery of healthcare services, including examination and consultations, over the telecommunication infrastructure. We use IoT and mobile application technology to transform primary healthcare system to a digital platform, providing safety to healthcare professionals and patients especially during the pandemic.

ICU Customization


Healthcare professionals need to have an all-time update for patients critically ill in ICU, which can be too tedious to do manually for multiple patients. To overcome this situation, we provide cross-platform application to monitor IoT based ICU monitoring system connected with our own BP, Pulse, SpO2 and temperature monitoring devices.

Home Automation Applications
